# U.S. Job Market — Humanoid Robotics Displacement 2025–2040

An interactive single-page visualization exploring how humanoid robotics and automation may reshape the U.S. labor market over the next 15 years.

## What It Shows

- **Robot cost vs. labor cost** — when robots become cheaper than human workers
- **Top 20 most displaced occupations** — projected % job loss by 2030, 2035, and 2040
- **Top 20 resilient & growing occupations** — roles likely to survive or expand
- **Three automation scenarios** — Slow, Baseline, and Fast adoption curves

## Data Sources

- U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) Occupational Outlook Handbook
- Boston Dynamics / Figure AI / Tesla Optimus robot cost projections
- Academic automation risk research (Frey & Osborne methodology)

## How To Use

Just open `job_market_viz.html` in any browser — no server, no dependencies, no install needed.

## Scenarios

| Scenario | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Slow** | Regulatory friction slows adoption |
| **Baseline** | Current trajectory continues |
| **Fast** | Rapid cost decline accelerates deployment |
